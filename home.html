Service

Decentralized

Most of the existing social networks use a centralized infrastructure where shared data is under the control and monitoring of the service provider on a limited number of servers.
We offer you Whisper, our new social network application, with a decentralized service architecture for social communication based on Blockchain technology, which allows users to maintain full control over their own data and identities.

Leverage the performance of cloud computing for a great experience

Social media platforms and cloud computing have led to massive personal data collection by big data giants, leading to user privacy concerns. Whisper is reshaping the way we use cloud computing in terms of user privacy and security.

* Independent of the cloud provider.
* Data is owned and controlled by users, not by service providers.
* Powered by Blockchain capabilities.

Safeguard Your Identity & Activities

Whisper enables you to encrypt all your activities (sharing content, chat, etc.) and keep your information private and secured in a decentralized network using the power of Blockchain technology. This means that all your personal information and activities will be kept private and no one can sell your personal data to a third party.

Full Encryption

All of your pieces of information is fully encrypted (End-to-End by user-owned encryption keys).

Share private content easily, without losing their ownership

We offer you the possibility to share any type of files with your friends easily and with total control of your own data.
